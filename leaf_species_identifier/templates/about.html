About Our Technology

How It Works

Our two-step process ensures accurate plant species identification

1

Leaf Validation

First, our system analyzes the uploaded image to determine if it contains a leaf. We use advanced computer vision techniques to validate leaf characteristics such as shape, texture, and color patterns.

2

Species Classification

If the image is validated as a leaf, our machine learning models analyze detailed features including geometric properties, vein patterns, margin characteristics, and texture to identify the plant species.
